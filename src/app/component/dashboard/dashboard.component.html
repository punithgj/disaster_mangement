How to Deal in Disaster Situations

Equip yourself with the knowledge and tools to stay safe and assist others during emergencies. Follow these essential steps:

  • Stay Calm: Keep a clear mind and avoid panic. Assess the situation before taking action.
  • Follow Alerts: Stay informed through official alerts and updates from local authorities.
  • Evacuation Plans: Familiarize yourself with evacuation routes and safe zones in your area.
  • Emergency Kit: Prepare a kit with essentials like water, food, first-aid, flashlight, and important documents.
  • Help Others: Assist children, elderly, and individuals with disabilities in reaching safe locations.
  • Stay Connected: Keep your phone charged and have backup communication devices available.
  • Report: Notify authorities about injuries, damages, or hazards in your area.
